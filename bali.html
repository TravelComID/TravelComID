Wonderfull of Bali Bali is a province in Indonesia which is located in the western part of the Nusa Tenggara Islands and has its capital in Denpasar City. Bali Island, which is the largest island in Bali Province, has several nicknames, including the Island of the Gods and the Island of a Thousand Temples. In 2020, the population of Bali province was 4,317,404 people, with a density of 747 people/km2, and by the end of 2023 it would be 4,344,554 people.
