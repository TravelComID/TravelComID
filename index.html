Bali is a province in Indonesia which is located in the western part of the Nusa Tenggara Islands and has its capital in Denpasar City. Bali Island, which is the largest island in Bali Province, has several nicknames, including the Island of the Gods and the Island of a Thousand Temples. In 2020, the population of Bali province was 4,317,404 people, with a density of 747 people/km2, and by the end of 2023 it would be 4,344,554 people.

Raja Ampat

Thousands of islands
Fantasy
Ocean

Raja Ampat Regency is one of the districts in the province of Southwest Papua, Indonesia. The capital of this district is located in Waisai, where Waisai is the administrative center of Raja Ampat Regency. This district has 610 islands, including the Raja Ampat archipelago. Four of them, namely Misool Island, Salawati, Batanta and Waigeo, are large islands.

Bintan Regency, previously known as Riau Islands Regency, is one of the districts in the Riau Islands province, Indonesia. The capital of the district is Bandar Seri Bentan or usually called Teluk Bintan sub-district. The population of Bintan district at the end of 2023 will be 175,873 people.
