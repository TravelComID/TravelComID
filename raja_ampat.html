The Thousand Of Islands Raja Ampat Regency is one of the districts in the province of Southwest Papua, Indonesia. The capital of this district is located in Waisai, where Waisai is the administrative center of Raja Ampat Regency. This district has 610 islands, including the Raja Ampat archipelago. Four of them, namely Misool Island, Salawati, Batanta and Waigeo, are large islands.
